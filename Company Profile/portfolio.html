02 / Featured Project

APO Theta Alumni
Service Foundation

Nonprofit Organization

The Alpha Phi Omega Theta Alumni Service Foundation Inc. (APO Theta ASFI) is a registered nonprofit uniting alumni from the Theta Chapter of Alpha Phi Omega International, rooted at the University of the Philippines Los Baños.

Their mission centers on three core principles — Leadership, Friendship, and Service — driving programs in reforestation, scholarships, and sustainable campus mobility.

Personal Brand & Website

Professor Derek Burton Collins is a Harvard-trained academic and cultural advisor based in Hong Kong, working at the intersection of scholarship, art, and commerce.

Former Senior Consultant to Phillips Asia, former Dean of the Faculty of Arts, and Founding Director of the Wong How Man Centre for Exploration at HKU — his work spans private advisory, auction houses, galleries, and museums across the globe.
